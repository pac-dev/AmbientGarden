Ambient.Garden: a landscape of generative music

Ambient.Garden is a music composition arranged in space rather than time. Moving through the space is moving though the music. The autopilot is a suggested path though the music, and can be disabled to freely explore.

Each tree links to the source code of its generative sound. This code can be experimented with to move deeper into the sound.

To protect the serene atmosphere, bicycles and jogging are not permitted within the Garden. Please respect the musical plants and wildlife, and leave no trace!
